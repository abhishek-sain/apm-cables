About APM Wires & Cables

APM Wires & Cables Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of premium electrical wires and cables in India. With over 25 years of industry experience, we specialize in producing high-quality electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

Our products are manufactured using 99.97% pure copper, ensuring superior conductivity and performance. All our wires and cables are IS 694 certified and meet stringent quality standards.
